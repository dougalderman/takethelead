About "Take The Lead"

Calm Owner, Calm Dog, Calm Home

“Take The Lead” offers an individualized guided approach to common dog walking problems. Our commonsense approach focuses on working on your dog’s behaviors where they occur the most, in your home and in your neighborhood. Our methods are fluid and flexible adapting to the owner's and dog's individuality.

With a calm owner and dog, clear communication, structure, and consistency, you can create the relationship that you want so that your dog will naturally follow your lead in everyday situations.

We at “Take The Lead” believe that there is not a one size fits all in working with your dog. We will assess the needs of your dog and work with you on a plan that will enhance your inner pack leader.

If you are looking for a dog who fits into your lifestyle and is a well-mannered, adjusted member of your family, look no further than "Take The Lead."

About The Trainer

Commonsense dog training for the real world

After working as a MSW clinical social worker for many years, Tina Alderman left her career for her dream of working with dogs. Along with owning a successful pet care company in Boston for over ten years, she volunteered at the local shelters and dog training facilities, increasing her skills and knowledge.

Tina rescued a high-energy, powerful cattle dog named Red from a local trainer. She realized that treats and obedience training weren't working for Red. Treats made Red more excited, and hours of exercising left him unfulfilled.

After attending Cesar Millan’s intensive Fundamentals of Dog Behavior class, it finally all came together for Tina. The most powerful tool was her own energy. “The way I projected my energy was directly affecting Red becoming a follower and ultimately, a happy, adjusted, member of our family.”

Tina says that she wasn’t born a natural leader but has been able to move out of her comfort zone, determined to fulfill her own pet’s needs and the needs of her clients and their dogs.

Tina moved to Fort Lauderdale in 2014 and volunteers at the "Training Cesar’s Way Fundamentals" course at The Country Inn Pet Resort in Fort Lauderdale.
