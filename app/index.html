About "Take The Lead"

“Take The Lead” offers an individualized guided approach to common dog walking problems. Our methods are fluid and flexible adapting to the owner's and dog's individuality.

With a calm owner and dog, clear communication, structure, and consistency, you can create the relationship that you want so that your dog will naturally follow you as his pack leader.

We at “Take The Lead” believe that there is not a one size fits all in dog training. We will assess the needs of your dog and work with you on a plan that will enhance your inner pack leader.

Our goal is to help you find the tools that work best for you and your dog and guide you towards a relationship where the dog will look to you for leadership in everyday life.
